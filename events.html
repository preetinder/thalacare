Latest Updates

News from ThalaCare India

Jun 2025

18,400 couples screened in Pre-Marital Drive — halfway to our 2025 goal

Our state-wide pre-marital screening programme has crossed the halfway mark, screening 18,400 couples so far. Of these, 2,210 individuals were identified as Thalassemia carriers, and 180 at-risk couples (where both partners are carriers) have been referred for genetic counselling.

May 2025

World Thalassemia Day 2025 — over 800 attend annual seminar

This year's World Thalassemia Day seminar at Balgandharva Rang Mandir drew over 800 attendees — our largest ever. Speakers included Dr. Vikram Shah (haematology) and Dr. Sunita Nair (genetic counselling), with a patient panel that drew a standing ovation.

Apr 2025

New partnership with Nashik Zilla Parishad for rural outreach

ThalaCare India has signed an MOU with the Nashik Zilla Parishad to conduct quarterly screening camps in rural Nashik district, with access to all PHC (Primary Health Centre) infrastructure. This partnership will extend our reach to an estimated 40,000 additional people over the next two years.
