Free Thalassemia Screening Camps

Walk in or register in advance. All tests — CBC, HPLC, and genetic counselling — are completely free of charge.

What to Expect

How our camps work

1

Arrive & Register

Walk in or register online. Bring a government ID. No appointment needed for walk-ins.

2

Blood Sample

A small blood sample is taken by our trained phlebotomists. The process takes under 5 minutes.

3

CBC & HPLC Test

Your sample is tested on-site for CBC. HPLC results are delivered within 24–48 hours by WhatsApp or email.

4

Counselling

If your results indicate carrier status, our genetic counsellors guide you on what this means for your family plans.
